Experience

I’m an aspiring web designer who loves everything about the web. I've lived in lots of different places and have worked in lots of different jobs. I’m excited to bring my life experience to the process of building fantastic looking websites.

I am 25 and have a positive approach to life, at the age of 21 I was diagnosed with an inherited genetic disease called Friedrich's Ataxia. My mobility deteriorated and I am now a wheelchair user. My original plan was to work in the motorsport industry and I studied motorsports mechanics at West Kent College, achieving a level 3 diploma at a merit grade. Soon after completing this I lost my mobility, so I had to put my thinking cap on and work out what I was going to do with my life and work. I have always been better at learning when i get myself stuck into a project rather than th theory side to learning. I can't wait to expand my skills using Treehouse.
